Using a Pixel 2 in 2024

 

TLDR: Somehow the Pixel 2 is almost 7 years old, but it's still a decent main in 2024 depending on your battery and software requirements

The screen - The 5 inch OLED screen on the Pixel 2 still has very good colour accuracy and looks very good under many weather conditions but it does attract a lot of fingerprints which can affect viewing experience.

 

The Camera - The 12MP camera still holds up very well and sometimes looks better than some cheap budget phones made in 2023/2024. The Pixel AI editing tools still hold up very well to make the image better or to remove objects, the video camera is decent but in 60fps for both 1080p and 4K looks very grainy in low light and lots of light

 

The Software - The phone came out with Android 8 and got upgrades till Android 11 which was longer than most androids that came out in the same year (e.g Galaxy S8 and Note 8). As of July 2024, Android 11 still supports most of the mainstream apps that exist in the google play store, and there are many custom roms made for this phone since it was a well known flagship.

 

The Battery - The Pixel 2's 2700mah is very low than cheap budget phones released in the last few years, but still it had amazing battery life when it was first released, and almost 7 years later it's still ok but specifically my battery shuts off when the camera is opened sometimes so it would probably need a replacement if you want to main it daily.

 

Thanks for reading my opinion on the Pixel 2 in 2024!
